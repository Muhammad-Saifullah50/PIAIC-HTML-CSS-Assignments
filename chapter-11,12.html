PIAIC

PRESIDENTIAL INITIATIVE FOR ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE AND COMPUTING

Preidential Initiative for Artificial Intelligence and Computing is a privately funded non-profit educational initiative started by the President of Pakistan, Dr Arif Alvi, Kazi Rahat Ali, and Zia Khan to help promote education, research and buisness oppurtunities in emerging tech fields like

  • Cloud Native Computing
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Blockchain
  • Internet of Things

Cloud Native Computing

The cloud native program prepares the students to progrm in open source technologies like Linux, Docker, Kubernetes, JavaScript Node JS, React and React Native while introducing the students to development of microservices and software development techniques on cloud platforms like AWS.

Artificial Intelligence

The Artificial Intelligence program of PIAIC takes the students through the foundations of the AI technology, via gteaching programming in open source technologies like Python, Numpy, Pandas, Tensorflow 2.0 and Flask, and teaching deploying practical AI solutions and usecases, and ending with real-life examples of modern day AI in practice.
